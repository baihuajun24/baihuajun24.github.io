Huajun Bai

Ph.D. Student, Tsinghua University

Efficient LLM Inference Systems · Speculative Decoding

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science & Technology at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Jiwu Shu in the Storage Research Group.


I build efficient inference systems for large language models, with a recent focus on speculative decoding and efficient reasoning — Tokenomics: getting the most useful work out of every token an LLM computes.


I also host MLSYS Feed, an AI-native podcast that briefs fresh MLSYS papers daily.
